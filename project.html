Project 1

MumLovesYou

MumLovesYou is a project based on the technology IFTTT. The project constructs a system that facilitates mum have better attention to the post on social media. The connection through platforms like instagram to email/Facebook helps mum always pay attention to their children. Besides, the video also show the intense relation between mum and children and how children de-surveilance of parents.

Project 3

Snack Tourism

Snack Tourism is a making space in which I try to build a fake tour city with well-known landmarks. I would like to use this project to explore on the topic of sanctification of life in which people are more care about fast style instead of paying attention to the quality and experience. So in the video, I try to use the printed landmarks, avatars, and customs to have this quick snack tourism.

Project 4

Playing with AR

In the project 4, I try to play with the technology of augmented reality. Based on the library AR Foundation and ARkit, I made this prototype in the Unity. In the object placement script, I mainly used the ray cast function to put the model into the physical space. Besides, I found it pretty interesting than when you look the reality through the virtual windows. The immerging of reality and virtuality really excites me and inspires me in the future project.
